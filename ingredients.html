OUR Process

Our Freeze-Dried Treats boast a simple and wholesome recipe featuring just three premium ingredients: cooked chicken liver, cooked sweet potato, and bone beef broth.

Chicken Liver: A powerhouse of nutrition, rich in protein, vitamins A and B12, iron, copper, and zinc. It's also an excellent source of omega-3 and omega-6 fatty acids.

Sweet Potato: Packed with fiber and essential vitamins like A (as beta-carotene), C, B vitamins, and potassium. It also provides valuable antioxidants.

Bone Beef Broth: A natural source of collagen, glucosamine, and chondroitin, supporting joint health.

Locally Sourced: All ingredients are sourced locally, ensuring the highest quality and freshness.
