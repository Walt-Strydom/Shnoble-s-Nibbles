We read the label so you don't have to.

Three ingredients. Nothing more. Every single one chosen for what it does for your dog — not for shelf life or cost-cutting.

What's in every treat

🍗

Cooked Chicken Liver

A powerhouse of nutrition, rich in protein, vitamins A and B12, iron, copper, and zinc. An excellent source of omega-3 and omega-6 fatty acids — loved by even the fussiest dogs.

🍠

Cooked Sweet Potato

Packed with fibre and essential vitamins — A (as beta-carotene), C, B vitamins, and potassium. Also provides valuable antioxidants that support your dog's immune system.

🦴

Bone Beef Broth

A natural source of collagen, glucosamine, and chondroitin. Supports joint health and adds a deep, irresistible flavour that dogs absolutely love.

Locally Sourced: All three ingredients are sourced locally from South African suppliers — ensuring the highest quality, freshness, and supporting local producers.

What you'll never find in our treats

We believe in transparency. These are a few of the things we'll never add — ever.

Artificial colours or dyes
Artificial flavours or flavour enhancers
Xylitol (highly toxic to dogs)
Propylene glycol
Corn syrup or added sugars
Mystery "meat by-products"
Chemical preservatives (BHA, BHT, ethoxyquin)
