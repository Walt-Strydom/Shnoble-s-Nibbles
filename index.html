Who are we?

"Shnoble's Nibbles makes premium dog treats with fresh, simple and natural, locally sourced ingredients. We handcraft all our treats using real meats and vegetables, without any unnecessary additives or preservatives. Our focus is on quality and wholesome goodness, offering your dog a healthy and delicious treat they'll love."

"Our treats are freeze-dried, a process where the treats are first frozen and then placed in a vacuum chamber. This removes the moisture from the treats directly as ice turns into vapor, preserving their natural flavor and nutrients without the use of heat or preservatives."
