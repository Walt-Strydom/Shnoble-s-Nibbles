Who we are

Shnoble's Nibbles makes premium dog treats with fresh, simple and natural, locally sourced ingredients. We handcraft all our treats using real meats and vegetables, without any unnecessary additives or preservatives.

Our treats are freeze-dried — a gentle process that locks in natural flavour and nutrients without heat or preservatives. Just clean, wholesome goodness in every bag.
