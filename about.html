Our Story....

It started with our two Bichons — Charlotte (Shnoble) and Rufus.

Like most dog parents, I wanted to give them the best — especially when it came to treats. But the more I read labels, the more frustrated I became. So many dog treats are full of fillers, preservatives, and ingredients I couldn't even pronounce.

I wanted something real. Something I’d actually feel good about giving them every day — whether after a walk, during training, or just because they’re cute.

So I began experimenting in my own kitchen. I started with what I knew was good: chicken liver, sweet potato, homemade beef bone broth. I learned how to freeze-dry the treats to lock in nutrients and keep them shelf-stable without adding anything artificial. And most importantly — Shnoble and Rufus loved them.

Friends and family noticed too — and started asking for treats for their dogs. That’s when I decided to turn this little idea into something real.

Now, I make every batch by hand, using simple, healthy ingredients and a lot of love. These treats are for dogs who deserve better — and for the humans who want to give them just that.
