🐾 Our Story

Made with Love.
Backed by Nature.

We started Shnoble's Nibbles because we couldn't find dog treats we truly trusted. So we made our own — and your dog is the winner.

The Beginning

One Dog. One Mission.
One Treat at a Time.

It started with Shnoble — our golden retriever who'd been battling skin issues for two years. After switching to a raw diet, we noticed a huge improvement, but treats were always the problem. Everything on the shelves was packed with artificial colours, preservatives, and mystery ingredients. So in 2020, out of pure love (and a bit of stubbornness), we baked our first batch of peanut butter biscuits in our home kitchen.

Our vet approved them. Shnoble devoured them. And when our neighbours started asking for bags for their dogs, we knew we were onto something special. What began as a kitchen experiment became a mission — to give every South African dog the wholesome treat they deserve.

Why Shnoble's?

The Gap in the SA Market

South Africa's pet food market is growing fast — but truly natural dog treats remain surprisingly rare. Most products on our shelves list "artificial antioxidants", "colour additives", and preservatives with names you can't pronounce. We dug into the research and found that many of these are linked to long-term health issues in dogs: skin irritation, digestive problems, and behavioural changes that owners often don't connect to their dog's diet.

Shnoble's Nibbles was built to fill that gap — using ingredients you'd find in your own kitchen. Every batch is small, every ingredient is intentional, and every treat is tested by the most important critics: your dogs. We believe that a treat should do more than taste good — it should genuinely contribute to a dog's wellbeing. That's not a marketing line. It's the reason we exist.

What We Stand For

Four Values. One Belief.

Everything we do is guided by four simple principles that keep us honest, purposeful, and deeply passionate about what we make.

Honesty

We tell you exactly what's in every treat. No hidden ingredients, no misleading claims. What you see on the label is what your dog gets — nothing more, nothing less.

Health

Every ingredient is chosen for its nutritional benefit. We research, consult with vets, and test before anything goes into our treats. Your dog's health is the bottom line.

Happiness

We make treats that dogs genuinely love. If the tail doesn't wag, we go back to the kitchen. Tail wags are our quality standard — and so far, they've never let us down.

Heart

We care about the people who trust us with their dogs' health. Your confidence in us is our greatest responsibility — one we take personally, every single batch.

Meet the Mascot

The Dog Behind the Brand

Shnoble (pronounced "SHNO-bull") is a 7-year-old golden retriever with the heart of a lion and the appetite of a small bear. He joined our family as a rescue puppy in 2018 and changed our lives completely — in ways we never expected and are profoundly grateful for.

He's the reason Shnoble's Nibbles exists. Every product goes through his discerning palate before it ever reaches a shop shelf. If Shnoble says no, it's back to the drawing board. So far, he's only rejected one recipe — and to be fair, it was our experimental kale and broccoli biscuit phase. We don't talk about that.

Fun fact: Shnoble once stole an entire batch of peanut butter biscuits off the cooling rack. We took it as a five-star review.
