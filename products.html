Why freeze-dried?

Not all preservation methods are equal. Freeze-drying is the gold standard for single-ingredient treats — and here's why we chose it.

🌡️

No Heat, No Damage

Traditional baking destroys heat-sensitive vitamins and nutrients. Freeze-drying removes moisture at low temperatures, keeping every nutrient fully intact.

🚫

No Preservatives Needed

Because all moisture is removed, freeze-dried treats stay shelf-stable without BHA, BHT, or any chemical preservative. See what we never add →

💥

Intense Natural Flavour

Freeze-drying concentrates the natural flavour of real meat and vegetables — giving your dog a treat that smells and tastes genuinely irresistible.
