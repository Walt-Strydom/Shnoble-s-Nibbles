Frequently Asked

How do I order? +

The easiest way is via WhatsApp. Send us a message with what you'd like and we'll confirm availability, pricing, and arrange delivery or collection.

Do you deliver? +

We're based in Randburg, Gauteng and currently deliver locally. Message us to discuss delivery to your area — we're always looking to expand our reach.

Are the treats safe for puppies? +

Our treats contain natural ingredients suitable for adult dogs. For puppies under 6 months, we recommend checking with your vet first. All ingredients are listed openly on our ingredients page.

Can I become a stockist? +

Yes! We welcome wholesale and reseller enquiries. Visit our stockists page or message us directly on WhatsApp with your business details.
